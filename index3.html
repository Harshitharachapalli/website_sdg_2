Global Hunger and Malnutrition Crisis

Action Against Hunger is a global humanitarian organization dedicated to saving lives and fighting hunger and malnutrition worldwide. We provide life-saving assistance to the most vulnerable populations affected by emergencies and long-term food insecurity.

Currently, over **828 million people** are affected by hunger globally, and our work has never been more vital. We deliver food, water, nutrition, and medical aid where it's needed most.

Emergency Assistance

25 MILLION people reached by Action Against Hunger through emergency relief in 2023.

6 MILLION women and children supported with malnutrition prevention and treatment programs in 2023.

48 HOURS was the average time for Action Against Hunger to provide emergency assistance in over 40 crises in 2023.

Impact of Action Against Hunger Worldwide

Since its founding, Action Against Hunger has helped millions of people across the globe with life-saving food, nutrition, and medical aid. With **4,000 staff** on the ground in over **45 countries**, we continue our fight against hunger and malnutrition.

We work with communities to build long-term solutions to food security and provide sustainable interventions for improving access to safe drinking water, food security, and health care.
