Hunger Crisis and Food Wastage in India

Feeding India by Zomato is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to tackling hunger and malnutrition. By rescuing surplus food and providing nutritious meals, we ensure that no one sleeps hungry.

With **189.2 million people** undernourished in India, Feeding India's mission is to bridge the gap between excess food and those in need, through sustainable food donation and meal programs.

Impact of Feeding India

Through our initiatives, we have been able to deliver millions of meals to those in need. We focus on reducing food waste, feeding the underprivileged, and supporting nutrition programs across India.
